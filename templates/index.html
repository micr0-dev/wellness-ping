What is this? (click to expand)

What this is: Regular email check-ins. If you don't respond, your emergency contacts get notified.

Who it's for: Anyone who needs someone to notice if they go silent.

  • Living alone (elderly, solo, remote workers)
  • Activists, journalists, researchers in risky situations
  • Hikers, travelers, adventurers
  • Anyone with health concerns or safety risks

How it works: Choose daily or weekly pings at your preferred time. Click the link in the email or reply "PONG" to confirm you're okay. Miss the ping? We'll send gentle reminders. Still no response by the deadline? Your emergency contacts get alerted.

Free forever. Open source.

Features (click to expand)

Core features:

  • Flexible scheduling: Daily or weekly check-ins at your preferred time
  • Gentle reminders: Multiple reminders before contacts are alerted
  • Multiple check-in methods: Click link or reply "PONG" to email
  • Custom alert messages: Add instructions for emergency contacts

Security features (optional):

  • PIN authentication: Require a PIN code to check in (prevents email spoofing)
  • Duress PIN: A second PIN that looks normal but secretly alerts contacts you're in danger

All security features are optional. The service works great with just email - add PINs only if you need extra protection.

Privacy & Security (click to expand)

What we store:

  • Your email address
  • Emergency contact emails
  • Check-in frequency and time preferences
  • Last check-in timestamp
  • Hashed PINs (if enabled - never stored in plaintext)

What we DON'T store:

  • No logs of check-ins or access
  • No IP addresses
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No passwords (email verification only)

Security measures:

  • Server hosted in Sweden with full disk encryption
  • SELinux security hardening
  • Session tokens expire after 30 minutes
  • Email provider (Postmark) only retains emails for 7 days
  • All data deleted when you stop the service
  • Open source code for transparency

Jurisdiction: Server in Sweden, operated from US. Provider (Finland). This makes legal requests complex and requires international cooperation.

Note: Built for reliability and security, but like any service, depends on email and internet connectivity. Have backup plans.
